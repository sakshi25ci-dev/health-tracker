About HealthPulse

Built for People,
Not for Patients

HealthPulse is a personal health management platform designed to empower individuals with better health awareness without replacing medical care.

HealthPulse was born from a simple belief: healthcare shouldn't be complicated. We built a tool that helps everyday people understand their symptoms, remember their medications, and track their health trends — all without needing medical training.

Our symptom checker uses a guided conversational approach (not AI diagnosis) to help you recognize patterns and decide when to seek professional care. Your medicine tracker keeps you adherent. Your dashboard keeps you informed.

⚠️ Important Medical Disclaimer

HealthPulse is not a medical device and does not provide medical diagnosis, advice, or treatment. All information is for educational purposes only. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making any medical decisions. In an emergency, call your local emergency services immediately.
